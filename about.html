About Us

SUNBIC was launched in 2022 as a higher education institution that offers intensive online undergraduate and graduate programs completely tuition-free.SUNBIC is the open university’s directorate with the mission to champion Indonesia by globalizing SUNBIC. SUNBIC Online University Open University is a Global directorate employs operational excellence and strategic clarity to enable All SUNBIC student through international engagement serving the community’s vision of “A world class knowledge university… in continuous pursuit of innovation and enterprise”. The core values of SUNBIC Online University Open University are Team Work, Discipline, Initiative. We believe that only working together diligently and in the spirit of continuous improvement we can achieve the change needed to accomplish the University’s vision.
